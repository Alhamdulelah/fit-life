Program Structure

Weeks 1-4: Foundation

  • Master proper form on all major lifts
  • Establish baseline strength levels
  • Full-body workouts 3x per week
  • Introduction to progressive overload
  • Focus on movement patterns

Weeks 5-8: Strength

  • Heavier weights with lower reps
  • 4-day upper/lower split
  • Increased volume on compound lifts
  • Introduction to advanced techniques
  • Strength testing benchmarks

Weeks 9-12: Hypertrophy

  • Increased training frequency
  • Body part specialization
  • Advanced intensity techniques
  • Peak contraction methods
  • Final strength assessments
